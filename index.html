Explanation of levels

How do you tell whether you're a beginner, intermediate or advanced lifter? To that we would say, great question! Many factors come into this such as how long you have been lifting.


Less than 6 months

you should consider the beggining programme as you most likely have little experience.

6 months to 2 years

you would be considered intermediate as you have gained a decent amount of muscle along with understanding correct form.

2 Years+

Advanced lifters are those who have been lifting more than 2 years and understand the mechanics of weightlifting along with having a significant amount of muscle. There are many more factors such as how much you can lift but if interested this will be provided within the links page.
